Projects

Here are some of the projects that I have worked on:

1. Neutron Stars in Modified Gravity

In this project, I worked with the Compact Object Astrophysics Group in my college (COAST) and studied modified gravity and how the equations for neutron stars (TOV) behave in this framework. I analyzed the case for R² gravity, where we added an αR² term to the action.

2. Oppenheimer-Snyder Collapse

This is an ongoing project for my GR course. Here I aim to explore the spherical collapse of matter into a blackhole. I will begin by exploring the simplest such model, the Oppenheimer Snyder Collapse before moving onto complex generalizations. One of the end goals of this project is to try and simulate this collapse using GR1D, a 1D spherically symmetric simulation software for General Relativistic simulations. You can read more about it in my blog :D
